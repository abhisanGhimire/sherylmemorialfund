SHERLY ELOISE BURR MEMORIAL FUND

The Sheryl Eloise Burr Memorial Fund is dedicated to continuing Momma Burr’s legacy of education, faith, and community service. This fund will provide financial assistance to college students pursuing higher education, embodying Sheryl’s passion for learning and her commitment to empowering future generations. Through this initiative, we honor her memory and lifelong dedication to making a difference in the lives of others.

Eligibility

To be eligible for the Sheryl Eloise Burr Memorial Fund scholarship, applicants must meet the following criteria

Currently enrolled

Be enrolled (or accepted for enrollment) as a full-time student in an accredited undergraduate or graduate program at a college or university.

GPA

Demonstrate a strong academic record with a minimum GPA of [Insert GPA - e.g., 3.0 on a 4.0 scale].

Essay

Submit a personal essay describing how they embody Sheryl Eloise Burr's legacy of education, faith, and community service.

Letter of recommendation

Provide two letters of recommendation from individuals who can attest to the applicant's character, academic achievements, and community involvement.

About

Scholarship Fund

The Sheryl Eloise Burr Memorial Fund is dedicated to honoring the legacy of "Momma Burr," a woman who embodied the values of education, faith, and community service. This scholarship provides financial assistance to college students who, like Sheryl, demonstrate a passion for learning and a commitment to making a positive impact on the world.

The Sheryl Eloise Burr Memorial Fund seeks to continue her legacy by investing in students who share her values. This scholarship aims to empower future generations to pursue their educational dreams and contribute to their communities, just as Sheryl did throughout her life.
